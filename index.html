Services
Investor Pitch Deck
A gripping story, contemporary visuals and robust business case are imperative to win over investors. We craft investor-grade pitch decks for startups to raise early-stage funds. We have worked with tech startups in verticals such as SaaS, Fintech, Consumer Internet, Health-tech, Marketing-tech, Ed-tech and other emerging sectors
Financicial Modelling & Analysis
We offer bespoke financial modelling and forecasting services for startups and investment firms. We construct comprehensive pro-forma projections for SaaS as well as non-tech sectors. Our startup financial models are user-friendly, well-structured and comprehensive.
Investment Research
We construct investor-grade business plans for raising early-stage capital for startups. So far, b-plans and memorandums prepared by us have helped our clients raise over $20 million. We also offer b-plan review service for startups/SMBs who have with existing plans
Business Plan Development
We offer independent valuation service. We conduct valuations of conventional business models as well as for early-stage startups. Our team has experience in conducting valuation for institutional investors as well as for startups
Pitch Deck Design
Our in-house designers are specialists in presentation and pitch deck design. The consultants work in tandem with the designers to ensure brevity and persuasiveness in the presentation decks. Our team designs decks for experienced Startup Founders, Management Consultants and leaders at Fortune 500.

What Makes
Gripping Story
Tell a story that investors can relate to. Pitch decks that follow a common, template-like approach are more likely to be ignored.
Accuracy of Information
Be optimistic, but don't exaggerate. Most of the experienced investors have knowledge of market, comps, and business models.
Business Case
Communicate the benefits to investors. Economic feasibility of a proposal plan is generally non-negotiable for seasoned investors.
Financial Plan
Business plan and financial model that projects your revenues, profits, and cash flows over 18-36 month period
Startup/Founder Background
Communicating why the right team is behind the startup. Demonstrate a good mix of experience and founder roles.
Design
Graphic design could be an important differentiator in the sea of pitch decks. Don't overkill though! You are not entering a graphic design competition.
